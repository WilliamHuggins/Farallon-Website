FARALLON

MINISTRY OF PLENTY

An immersive, cyber-noir experience for the generative AI music artist Farallon. Farallon explores the intersection of human emotion and machine learning, creating a sound that sits between algorithmic precision and human vulnerability.

The new album "Ministry of Plenty" is out now, channeling an 80's dystopian San Francisco inspired by George Orwell's 1984. Farallon is a musical project exploring the concept of a digital entity with a composite memory of San Francisco.
